In Germany, if you finish a lecture and start clapping, you might find yourself in a very quiet room with a few confused looks. Instead, you’ll hear a rhythmic, thundering **knock on the wooden desks**.

This tradition, known as ***Akademisches Klopfen*** (academic knocking), is a quintessential part of German university life.
